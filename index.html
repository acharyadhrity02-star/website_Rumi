Professional Summary

Academic-oriented overview for applications, collaborations, and lab positions.

Multidisciplinary researcher specializing in ion-doped bioceramics and electro/magneto-responsive hydrogel systems for bone and soft-tissue regeneration. Experienced in 3D bioprinting (DIW/DLP), nanocomposite design, microneedle platforms, and advanced cellular assays.

Research spans controlled ionic microenvironments, antibacterial/antibiofilm scaffolds, and mechanotransduction modulation (e.g., YAP/TAZ, tight junction pathways) using stimulus-enabled bioreactor approaches.

Research Focus

  • Ion-doped ceramic/hydrogel hybrids for antibacterial bone regeneration
  • Stimulus-responsive hydrogels (electrical / magnetic) for cell control
  • Mechanotransduction and barrier signaling (YAP/TAZ, tight junction pathways)
  • DIW/DLP bioprinting: formulation ‚Üí printing ‚Üí crosslinking ‚Üí characterization

Key Methods

  • Material characterization: SEM/EDS, FTIR, XRD

  • Rheology, mechanical testing, print fidelity evaluation
  • Cell assays: cytocompatibility, migration, differentiation, imaging
