Professional Summary

Multidisciplinary researcher specializing in ion-doped bioceramics and electro/magneto-responsive hydrogel systems for bone and soft-tissue regeneration. Experienced in 3D bioprinting (DIW/DLP), nanocomposite design, microneedle platforms, and advanced cellular assays.

Research focus spans controlled ionic microenvironments, antibacterial/antibiofilm scaffolds, and mechanotransduction modulation (e.g., YAP/TAZ, tight junction pathways) using stimulus-enabled bioreactor approaches.
